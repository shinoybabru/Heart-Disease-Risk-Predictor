🩺 Top Prevention Recommendations

🚭Quit Smoking: Reduces heart disease risk by up to 50% within 1 year of quitting.
🏃Exercise Regularly: 150 minutes/week of moderate activity reduces risk by 30-35%.
🥗Heart-Healthy Diet: Mediterranean diet reduces cardiovascular events by 30%.
⚖️Maintain Healthy BMI: Keeping BMI below 25 significantly reduces strain on the heart.
🧘Manage Stress: Chronic stress raises BP and cortisol — practice mindfulness or yoga.
🍺Limit Alcohol: Heavy drinking raises BP and weakens the heart muscle over time.
💊Control BP & Cholesterol: Regular monitoring and medication adherence are critical.
🩸Manage Diabetes: Keeping HbA1c below 7% reduces cardiovascular complications.
